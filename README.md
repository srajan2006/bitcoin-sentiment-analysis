# Bitcoin Market Sentiment vs Trader Performance Analysis

## Objective

Analyze the relationship between Bitcoin market sentiment (Fear & Greed Index) and trader performance using historical Hyperliquid trading data.

## Datasets

1. Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index Dataset : https://drive.google.com/file/d/1PgQC0tO8XN-wqkNyghWc_-mnrYv_nhSf/view
2. Hyperliquid Historical Trader Dataset : https://drive.google.com/file/d/1IAfLZwu6rJzyWKgBToqwSmmVYU6VbjVs/view

## Methodology

* Data Cleaning
* Feature Engineering
* Date Alignment
* Dataset Merging
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Sentiment-Based Performance Analysis

## Key Findings

### Average Profit by Sentiment

* Extreme Greed produced the highest average profit per trade (67.89).

### Total Profit

* Fear generated the highest total profit (3.36M).

### Trading Activity

* Fear periods recorded the highest number of trades.

### Market Behavior

* Traders performed better in strongly directional markets than in neutral conditions.

Market sentiment significantly influences trader behavior and profitability. Incorporating sentiment indicators into trading strategies may improve decision-making and risk management.
